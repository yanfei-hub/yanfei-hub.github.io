This page highlights selected publications that reflect my current research direction in causal inference, target trial emulation, and longitudinal clinical modeling. For a full and updated list, please see my [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=gF1N-WgAAAAJ&hl=en).

## Featured publications

### An operational target trial simulation framework for clinical information using electronic health records
**Yanfei Wang, Yi Guo, Yongqiu Li, Tuo Lin.** 2026. *npj Digital Medicine*.  
An operational framework for reconstructing clinically meaningful target trial objects from routine EHR data, with emphasis on action reconstruction, decision-time alignment, and empirical support.  
[DOI](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41746-026-02563-z) | [Code](https://github.com/yanfei-hub/EHR-Target-Trail-Emulation)

### Integration of Genetic and Imaging Data for Alzheimer's Disease Diagnosis and Interpretation
**Wang Y., Wang Q., Zhou M., et al.** 2025. *Advanced Science*, 12(41), e07629.  
A multimodal framework integrating genetic and imaging information for Alzheimer’s disease diagnosis and interpretation.

### A real-world cohort study of immune-related adverse events in patients receiving immune checkpoint inhibitors
**Wang Y., Guo Y., Tan A. C., et al.** 2025. *npj Precision Oncology*, 9(1), 346.  
A real-world analysis of immune-related adverse events in patients treated with immune checkpoint inhibitors.

### Revealing Chronic Disease Progression Patterns Using Gaussian Process for Stage Inference
**Wang Y., Zhou X., et al.** 2023. *Journal of the American Medical Informatics Association*.  
A longitudinal modeling study using Gaussian process methods to characterize chronic disease progression patterns.
